Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna soup takes all the best parts of lasagna with a quarter of the effort a real lasagna takes.

Ingredients

  • 10 small meatballs raw or prepared thawed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup onion finely chopped
  • ½ cup green bell pepper finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons garlic minced
  • ½ tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 14.5 oz (1 can) tomato sauce
  • 8 oz (1 can) diced tomatoes
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • 10 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • ½ cup cottage cheese
  • fresh basil for topping
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese shredded
  • 4 slices fresh mozzarella ball
